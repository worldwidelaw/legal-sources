# INTL/IMFAT — IMF Administrative Tribunal Judgments

The IMF Administrative Tribunal (IMFAT) is an independent judicial body established
by the IMF Board of Governors for the resolution of employment disputes between the
International Monetary Fund and its staff members.

- **Coverage**: 61 judgments (2006–present)
- **Format**: PDF documents with full text extraction
- **Language**: English
- **Source**: https://www.imf.org/en/about/imfat-index/imfat-judgements
