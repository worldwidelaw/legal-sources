# KE/CBK-PrudentialGuidelines

Central Bank of Kenya — Prudential Guidelines and Regulatory Documents

## Overview

Fetches prudential guidelines, risk management guidelines, AML/CFT guidance notes,
banking regulations, and circulars from the Central Bank of Kenya website.

## Data

- ~70 regulatory PDF documents
- Language: English
- Coverage: 2006 to present
- Document types: Legislation, prudential guidelines, guidance notes, regulations, circulars

[Kenya Open Government Data](https://www.centralbank.go.ke/) — Government regulatory documents are public information.
