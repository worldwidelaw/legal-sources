# XK/ATK — Kosovo Tax Administration

Tax guidelines, administrative instructions, laws, and informative materials from the Kosovo Tax Administration (Administrata Tatimore e Kosovës).

## Coverage

- **Guidelines & Regulations**: Administrative instructions, internal regulations, manuals for tax procedures, VAT refunds, tax certificates
- **Tax Laws**: VAT Law, Corporate Income Tax Law, Personal Income Tax Law, Tax Procedures Law, Public Debt Forgiveness Law
- **Informative Materials**: Taxpayer seminars, fiscal compliance changes, informational bulletins

## Data Access

Documents are published as English-language PDFs on the ATK WordPress site:
- https://www.atk-ks.org/en/udhezues-manuale-dhe-rregullore/
- https://www.atk-ks.org/en/legislation/laws/
- https://www.atk-ks.org/en/materiale-informative/

Full text is extracted from PDFs using the project's common PDF extraction pipeline.
