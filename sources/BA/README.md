# Bosnia and Herzegovina — Legal Data Sources

> **Last updated:** 2026-02-21

## Overview

Bosnia and Herzegovina is a **civil law** country with a **complex federal structure**: the **Federation of BiH** (10 cantons) and **Republika Srpska**, plus the **Brcko District**. Each entity has its own legislation, courts, and legal system.

## Consolidated Legislation vs. Official Journal

Bosnia does **not** have consolidated legislation. Multiple official gazettes exist at state, entity, cantonal, and district levels.
