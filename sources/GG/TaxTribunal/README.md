## Why this source is blocked

**Category:** decisions_not_public

**Technical reason:** `decisions_not_public`

**Details:** Independent body hearing appeals under Income Tax (Guernsey) Law 1975. Formerly Guernsey Tax Tribunal (renamed 1 Jan 2022). Decisions are NOT generally made public because they contain confidential taxpayer information. Cannot scrape.
