# TW/MOF-TaxInterpretations

Taiwan Ministry of Finance Tax Interpretations (財政部各稅法令函釋檢索系統)

## Source

Official tax interpretive rulings from the MOF Tax Law Interpretation Search System at [ttc.mof.gov.tw](https://ttc.mof.gov.tw/). Covers 18 tax acts including Income Tax, Business Tax, Land Tax, Estate & Gift Tax, Customs, and more.

## Data

- **Type:** doctrine (tax interpretations / rulings)
- **Language:** Chinese (Traditional)
- **API:** POST endpoints at ttc.mof.gov.tw/Api/ — no authentication required
- **Records:** Thousands of interpretive rulings across all tax categories
