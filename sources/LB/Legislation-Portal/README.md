# Lebanon Legislation Portal

**Source:** [https://www.legallaw.ul.edu.lb/](https://www.legallaw.ul.edu.lb/)
**Country:** LB
**Data types:** legislation
**Status:** Blocked

## Why this source is blocked

**Category:** Subscription or payment required

**Technical reason:** `no_api_subscription_required`

**Details:** No public API. Official Gazette (jo.pcm.gov.lb) requires paid subscription. Lebanese University Legal Informatics Center (legallaw.ul.edu.lb) is web-only with no API. No open data portal for legislation. Constitutional Council (cc.gov.lb) web-only.
