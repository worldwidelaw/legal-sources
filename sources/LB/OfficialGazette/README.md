## Why this source is blocked

**Category:** Subscription or payment required

**Technical reason:** `subscription_required`

**Details:** From issue #228. Official Gazette from Presidency of Council of Ministers. Requires paid subscription (10M or 5M LBP annual cards via LibanPost). No public API. Login required for all content.
