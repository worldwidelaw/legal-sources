# JP/NTA-AdvanceRulings — Japan NTA Written Response Examples (文書回答事例)

Advance rulings issued by the Japan National Tax Agency (国税庁) in response
to formal taxpayer inquiries. ~712 rulings across 9 tax categories:

- Income Tax (所得税)
- Withholding Tax (源泉所得税)
- Capital Gains & Forestry (譲渡所得・山林所得)
- Inheritance Tax (相続税)
- Gift Tax (贈与税)
- Property Valuation (財産評価)
- Corporate Tax (法人税)
- Consumption Tax (消費税)
- Other Taxes (諸税)

Each ruling includes the formal inquiry, NTA's official response, and
detailed appendix analysis with legal reasoning.
