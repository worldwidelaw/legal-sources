# RO/ICCJ — Înalta Curte de Casație și Justiție

Romanian High Court of Cassation and Justice — Romania's supreme court.

**URL:** https://www.scj.ro
**Data type:** case_law
**Auth:** none
**Records:** ~235,000 decisions

Decisions are accessed via sequential detail pages on scj.ro. Each page
contains the full text of the decision in HTML, including the court section,
bench composition, decision number, and the complete ruling.

## Coverage

- All court sections: Civil I, Civil II, Criminal, Administrative & Fiscal
- Panels of 5 and 9 judges
- RIL (Recurs în Interesul Legii) — appeals in the interest of the law
- Preliminary rulings (Hotărâri Prealabile)
