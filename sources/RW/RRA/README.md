# RW/RRA — Rwanda Revenue Authority

Tax laws, presidential orders, ministerial orders, commissioner general's rules, public rulings, and VAT exemption lists from the Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA).

## Coverage

- **Tax Laws**: Income Tax, VAT, Excise Duty, Environmental Levy, Tourism Tax, Mining Tax
- **Presidential Orders**: Tax implementation orders
- **Ministerial Orders**: Tax regulation orders
- **Commissioner General's Rules & Public Rulings**: Administrative guidance
- **VAT Exemption Lists**: Approved pharmaceutical, educational, agricultural, and energy exemptions

## Data Access

Documents are published as trilingual (Kinyarwanda/English/French) PDFs on:
- https://www.rra.gov.rw/en/laws-policies-and-rulings

Full text is extracted from PDFs using the project's common PDF extraction pipeline.
