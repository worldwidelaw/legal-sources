# Andorra — Legal Data Sources

> **Last updated:** 2026-02-21

## Overview

Andorra is a **co-principality** (French President + Bishop of Urgell) with a **civil law** system influenced by Catalan, French, and Spanish law. Very small jurisdiction, 7 parishes. All legal texts in **Catalan**.

## Consolidated Legislation vs. Official Journal

Andorra does **not** have consolidated legislation. BOPA is the sole official publication.

## Sub-jurisdictions

7 parishes (parroquies) with limited regulatory power. No separate legal databases.

## How to Contribute

Priority: court decisions, AFA circulars, historical BOPA (pre-2015). Create directories under `sources/AD/[SourceName]/`.
