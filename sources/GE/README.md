## Overview

Georgia is a **civil law** country, **unitary** (Abkhazia and South Ossetia de facto separate). EU candidate country. Provides **consolidated legislation** through **Matsne** (matsne.gov.ge) in Georgian, English, and Russian.

## Consolidated Legislation vs. Official Journal

**Consolidated** via Matsne (matsne.gov.ge), available in Georgian, English, and Russian.

## Sub-jurisdictions

**Unitary**. Abkhazia and South Ossetia operate de facto separate legal systems (not indexed). Adjara has autonomous republic status within Georgia.
