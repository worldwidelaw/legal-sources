## Consolidated Legislation vs. Official Journal

Ukraine provides legislation through the Verkhovna Rada portal. Vidomosti Verkhovnoi Rady is the official gazette.

## Sub-jurisdictions

**Unitary state**. Crimea and parts of Donetsk/Luhansk oblasts are under occupation. The Autonomous Republic of Crimea has constitutional autonomy (currently occupied).
