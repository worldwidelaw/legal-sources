# TH/OTCC — Thailand Trade Competition Commission Verdicts

Competition enforcement verdicts from the Trade Competition Commission of Thailand (TCCT, formerly OTCC). Covers unfair trade practices, price-fixing, cartel agreements, market abuse, franchise disputes, and exclusive dealing decisions.

Full text is extracted from PDF decisions published on the TCCT website. Content is in Thai.

- **Source**: [TCCT Verdict Page](https://www.tcct.or.th/view/1/Verdict/TH-TH)
- **Coverage**: ~126 verdicts (2019–2026)
- **Language**: Thai
- **Update frequency**: Ongoing as new decisions are published
