# NP/IRD-TaxGuidance — Nepal Inland Revenue Department Tax Guidance

Tax acts, rules & regulations, directives, procedures, and notices/circulars
published by Nepal's Inland Revenue Department (IRD) at [ird.gov.np](https://ird.gov.np/).

## Coverage

| Category | Items | Description |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Acts | ~5 | Income Tax Act, VAT Act, Excise Act, Liquor Act |
| Rules & Regulations | ~18 | Income tax rules, VAT rules, excise rules |
| Directives | ~18 | Transfer pricing, AML/CFT, beneficial ownership, DPMS |
| Procedures | ~18 | Digital service tax, luxury tax, e-invoice procedures |
| Notices | ~60 | Tax relief, compliance guidance, circulars |

**Total: ~100+ documents** with full text extracted from embedded PDFs.

## Data Type

`doctrine` — official tax guidance and interpretive documents.

## Language

Primarily **Nepali** with some documents in **English** (e.g., TFS Guidelines,
Transfer Pricing Directives).
