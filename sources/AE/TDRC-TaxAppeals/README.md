# UAE Tax Disputes Resolution Committee Decisions

**Source:** [https://www.tax.gov.ae](https://www.tax.gov.ae)
**Country:** AE
**Data types:** case_law
**Status:** Blocked

## Why this source is blocked

**Category:** no_public_access

**Technical reason:** `no_public_access`

**Details:** TDRC decisions are not publicly published. Protected by Article 7 of Tax Procedures Law (confidentiality). Communicated only to parties (taxpayer + FTA). No portal, API, or database. Only known indirectly through law firm summaries and Federal Court appeals.
