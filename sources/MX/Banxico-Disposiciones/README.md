# MX/Banxico-Disposiciones

**Banco de México — Disposiciones y Circulares**

Regulatory circulars and general provisions issued by Mexico's central bank
(Banco de México) covering credit institutions, payment systems, foreign
exchange operations, interest rates, commissions, and financial operations.

- **Source**: https://www.banxico.org.mx/marco-normativo/normativa-agrupada-por-ano-cr.html
- **Type**: doctrine
- **Volume**: ~319 unique regulations (1969–present)
- **Language**: Spanish
- **Format**: PDF (compiled/consolidated text)
