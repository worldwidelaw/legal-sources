# CL/SERNAC — Servicio Nacional del Consumidor

Consumer protection doctrine from Chile's National Consumer Service (SERNAC).

## Coverage

- **Circulares Interpretativas** (~41 documents, 2019–present): general guidance on consumer protection law interpretation
- **Dictámenes Interpretativos** (~85 documents, 2020–present): specific interpretive rulings on consumer law questions

## Data Source

Documents are scraped from two listing pages on SERNAC's public portal:
- Circulares: https://www.sernac.cl/portal/618/w3-propertyvalue-21072.html
- Dictámenes: https://www.sernac.cl/portal/618/w3-propertyvalue-66262.html

Full text is extracted from PDFs published alongside each document.
