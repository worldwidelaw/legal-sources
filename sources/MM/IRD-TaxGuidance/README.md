# MM/IRD-TaxGuidance

Myanmar Internal Revenue Department (IRD) Digital Library — tax laws, interpretations, rulings, directives, public notices, and pamphlets.

## Source

- **URL**: https://digitallibrary.ird.gov.mm/
- **Data type**: Doctrine
- **Format**: PDF documents (Burmese and English)
- **Documents**: ~200 across three collections
- **Coverage**: Tax laws (2011–2025), rulings/directives (2016–2024), pamphlets

## Collections

| Collection | Page | Count | Download pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tax Laws | policyandlaw.php | ~33 | download_file.php?id=XX |
| Interpretations & Rulings | policy.php | ~116 | downloadp.php?id=XX |
| Pamphlets | pamphlet.php | ~54 | pamphletdownload.php?id=XX |
