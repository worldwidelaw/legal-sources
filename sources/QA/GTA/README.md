# QA/GTA — Qatar General Tax Authority

Tax circulars, decisions, and laws from the Qatar General Tax Authority (GTA).

## Coverage

- **Circulars**: Tax circulars from the GTA President (e.g., tax card issuance, e-filing requirements, withholding tax registration)
- **Decisions**: Emiri decisions, ministerial resolutions, and GTA presidential decisions on tax matters
- **Laws**: Income Tax Law, Excise Tax Law, amendments, and Pillar Two (Global Minimum Tax) regulations

## Data Access

Documents are published as English-language PDFs on gta.gov.qa across three sections:
- https://gta.gov.qa/en/circulars
- https://gta.gov.qa/en/decisions
- https://gta.gov.qa/en/laws

Full text is extracted from PDFs using the project's common PDF extraction pipeline.
